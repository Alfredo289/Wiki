General manager of Cora, Every.to’s AI email assistant. Creator of compound-engineering, an AI-native engineering philosophy with 14,000+ GitHub stars. Sole engineer building Cora.

Known for the folder-as-agent pattern - the claim that a project folder with accumulated context is the real “agent,” not the model. Runs 44 such folder-agents with a custom dispatch layer. Primarily uses Claude Opus 4.6, also GPT 5.4 and Gemini Pro 3.1.

Published at Every.to’s Source Code column. Collaborates with product leader Trevin Chow on compound engineering workshops.
