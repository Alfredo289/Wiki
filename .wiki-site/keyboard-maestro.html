macOS automation application for creating keyboard-triggered macros and complex workflows. Used by Brett Terpstra as part of his keyboard-driven-workflow for automating repetitive tasks and streamlining text editing. Website: keyboardmaestro.com.

Combined with Karabiner-Elements for key remapping and DefaultKeyBindings.dict for system-level shortcuts, forms the modification layer of a keyboard-driven setup.
