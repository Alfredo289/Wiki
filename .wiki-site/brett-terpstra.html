Developer and writer based in Minnesota, USA. Maintains a blog at brettterpstra.com focused on macOS productivity tools and automation. Active on GitHub (ttscoff) and Mastodon.

Notable projects: NiftyMenu (menu item scanner), Cheaters (cheatsheet viewer), custom DefaultKeyBindings.dict for macOS, Karabiner-Elements mods.

Known for his extensive keyboard-driven-workflow combining 20+ macOS utilities. Key tools in his daily stack: LaunchBar, Keyboard Maestro, Karabiner-Elements, BetterTouchTool, KindaVim, Superkey, Moom, Vimium, Cotypist.
