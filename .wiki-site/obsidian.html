Markdown-based knowledge management application. In the context of llm-wikid, serves as the UI layer for browsing the wiki - graph view for visualizing connections, Dataview for structured queries, and Web Clipper for collecting sources from the browser into raw/clippings/.

Recommended plugins (from llm-wikid): Dataview, Obsidian Git, Templater, Tag Wrangler.

Additional plugins recommended by YB (yb-effect-2026-commonplace-practice): Copilot, Readwise, Excalidraw, Simple Note Review.

As a Personal Thinking Tool

YB describes using Obsidian + Claude as a personal thinking system built around five practices: commonplace-practice, atomic-notes, intentional-reading, agentic-constitution, and knowledge-work-prs. This use case differs from the llm-wiki-pattern research workflow - the emphasis is on capturing personal context that AI can’t generate on its own.
